Getting Started with WhatsappKhata

WhatsappKhata makes expense tracking as simple as sending a WhatsApp message. No need to download additional apps or remember complex procedures. Simply send your expense as a message and let our AI-powered system handle the categorization and tracking for you. Start by registering your WhatsApp number on our platform and begin your journey towards better financial management.

Simple Message Format

Sending expense information is incredibly easy. Just type the item name followed by the amount. For example: "Apple 50" or "Coffee 120". Our intelligent system will automatically understand and categorize your expense. You can send multiple expenses in separate messages, and each will be processed individually. The system recognizes various formats and currencies, making it flexible for different users.

Understanding Your Daily Reports

Every day at your chosen time, WhatsappKhata sends you a comprehensive summary of your expenses. The report includes your total spending, category-wise breakdown, and insights into your spending patterns. You can customize when you receive these reports to fit your schedule. The reports help you stay aware of your spending habits and make informed financial decisions.
